1. **Download the APK:**

2. **Enable Unknown Sources:**
   On your Android device, go to **Settings** > **Security** and enable **Unknown Sources**. This allows you to install apps from outside the Google Play Store.

3. **Install the APK:**
   Open the downloaded APK file and follow the prompts to install the app.

4. **Open the App:**
   Once installed, find the app in your app drawer and tap to open.

5. **Set Up Your Account:**

6. **Start Collecting Data:**
